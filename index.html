Hi, I am Camila.

Cartographer. Architect and urbanist. Entrepreneur.

From Argentina, nowadays living in Germany.
Never enough travel, never enough mountains, never enough maps.
During my last years of university I discover, through urbanism, the art of map making.
I totally felt in love and I started training myself in the field.
After 3 years of courses, self-learning and after co-founding Mapoteca,
I decided to study a Master in Cartography which I am doing nowadays.
Beside my studies I work as a freelancer cartographer making beautiful maps that tell stories.
