Mapoteca

In April 2018, I co-founded Mapoteca, an illustrated map online shop and store
based in Buenos Aires, Argentina.

In Mapoteca we work with illustrators, designers and artists.
With their creativity and imagination they transform our maps in art and design pieces
for decoration and to inspire people for having always their backpack ready to travel.
