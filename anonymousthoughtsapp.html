Anonymous Thoughts app

App coded with java.
Group project for TU Dresden, 2023.

A map interface where for people to share their thoughts about certain locations with others.
This thoughts can be about general feelings a user have at a random location, or known places.
The idea is for users to share their thouthgs but also for others, tourists or anyone, to experience an area through the thoughts of others.
The thoughts can be categorized by specific feelings.
