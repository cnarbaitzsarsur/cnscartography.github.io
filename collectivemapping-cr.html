Collective mapping: Club de reparadores

Workshop coordination with Urbanismo Vivo.
The idea of the workshop was to connect young people with their local community and to document the social fabric of the neighborhood. For this, we worked with high school students in a collective mapping project focused on neighborhood repair shops and their stories.
cheLA - TaMaCo, 2019.
