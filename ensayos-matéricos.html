Ensayos Matéricos: WORKSHOP DE CONSTRUCCIÓN 1:1

I worked on the workshop organization and coordination. The workshop was designed for university students from Argentina and Paraguay. The idea was to provide the space to understand the construction processes, the relationship between materials, weight and structure, to come into contact with the tools and thus to experience the built architectural space.
TaMaCo and Colectivo Aqua Alta, 2019.
