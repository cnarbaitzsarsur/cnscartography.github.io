Hi, I am Camila. Cartographer. Architect and urbanist. Entrepreneur.

From Argentina, nowadays living between Germany and Austria.
Never enough travel, never enough mountains, never enough maps.

I was supposed to be an architect but during my last years of university I discover, through urbanism, the art of map making. I totally felt in love with it and, then, I started training myself in the field. After 3 years of courses, self-learning and after co-founding Mapoteca, I decided to study a Master in Cartography which I am doing nowadays.

Beside cartography, I am a creative, I love painting, hiking and getting lost by wandering around cities.
